### STEP 2: View the Dashboard (Home Screen)

After login, you will see the **Dashboard** with:

#### üîµ **Previous Shift Handover** (Blue Box at Top)
- This shows important information from the previous shift
- Read this FIRST when starting your shift
- Example: "Press Shop Line 1 running smoothly, new coil installed..."

#### üìä **Quick Stats** (4 Boxes)
- Current Shift: Morning Shift (06:00 - 14:00)
- Pending Tasks: Number of tasks to complete
- Active Alerts: Important warnings
- Machines Online: How many machines are working

#### üéØ **What to Do:**
- Read the Previous Shift Handover information (blue box)
- Check how many machines are running
- Look for any alerts or pending tasks

---

### STEP 3: View All Manufacturing Operations

1. Click **"Logbook"** in the left menu
2. You will see all manufacturing activities

#### üìã **What You'll See:**
- **Department Filter Dropdown** - Filter by:
  - Press Shop
  - Body Shop
  - Paint Shop
  - Assembly Shop
  - Quality
  - Maintenance
  - Safety & Environment

#### üîç **How to Filter:**
1. Click the dropdown that says "All Departments"
2. Select "Press Shop" (or any department)
3. You'll see only entries for that department

---

### STEP 4: Add a New Log Entry

1. On the Logbook page, click the green **"Add Entry"** button
2. Fill in the form:

#### üìù **Example Entry for Press Shop:**

**Department:** Press Shop  
**Area:** Stamping Line 1  
**Task Description:**
```
Completed hourly quality inspection - all panels within specification
```
**Priority:** Medium  
**Remarks:**
```
Checked 50 panels. Surface finish excellent. No defects found. 
Next inspection due at 10:00 AM.
```

3. Click blue **"Submit Entry"** button
4. You'll see a green success message!

---

### STEP 5: View Handover Information

1. Click **"Handover"** in the left menu
2. You will see:
   - **Pending Handovers** (Orange) - Need approval
   - **Recent Approved Handovers** (Green) - Already approved

#### üîÑ **What This Means:**
- **Green handovers** = Information from previous shift
- **Orange handovers** = Waiting for supervisor approval
- Read all handover details to know what happened before your shift
